{% blocktrans %} The Bus Open Data Service (BODS) is the central service for publishing high quality information for all local bus services in England. The Bus Services Act 2017 requires operators of local bus services in England to publish data to the Bus Open Data Service. There are three types of data that operators must published in certain data formats: timetables, bus location and fares. {% endblocktrans %}
