{% trans 'Promoting the digital channel' %}

{% blocktrans %} The new Bus Open Data Service makes it easier than ever before to submit information digitally - see the {% endblocktrans %} operator guidance {% blocktrans %} . It includes links to low cost software for small operators to produce digital timetable information, as well as a free public spreadsheet template for small operators to use. Please emphasise to small operators in your area that the digital channel will be cheaper, easier and faster for them than posting in paper forms. You may wish to include this recommendation in letters and emails that you send to bus operators in your area, including hyperlinks to the new service. {% endblocktrans %}
